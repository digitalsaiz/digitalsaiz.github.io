How It Works

From Session to Signed Note in 4 Steps

No templates to fill. No copy-pasting. Just answer a few quick questions and let AI do the rest.

1

Open the App

Tap New Note from the home screen. Start a note any time — even mid-session.

2

Walk the Wizard

Provider, recipient, service type, location, date, and a short questionnaire. Takes under 2 minutes.

3

Tap Generate

AI writes a professional 2–4 paragraph note in seconds. Specific, clinical, and audit-ready.

4

Review & Share

Edit if needed, then share or save to the cloud. Notes stay searchable in your history.
